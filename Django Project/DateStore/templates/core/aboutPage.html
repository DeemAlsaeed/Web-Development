About Sukri&Kalas

At Sukri&Kalas, we're passionate about bringing you the finest selection of dates from around the world.
Whether you're a seasoned date connoisseur or new to the world of this delectable fruit, we're here to guide you on a journey of taste and discovery.

Founded in 2024, Sukri&Kalas was born out of a love for dates and a desire to share their incredible flavors with the world. Our founder, Deem, grew up surrounded by date palms in AlQassim, where dates were not just a fruit but a way of life. Inspired by this rich heritage, Sukri&Kalas set out to create a platform that celebrates the diversity and versatility of dates.
